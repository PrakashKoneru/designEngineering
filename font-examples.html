Head-to-Head Comparison

Source Sans Pro

Pros: Industry standard, highly professional, excellent readability, trusted by medical institutions

Cons: Requires Adobe Fonts subscription, can feel slightly cold

Nunito Sans

Pros: Free, warm & approachable, reduces patient anxiety, modern rounded design

Cons: May be too casual for some medical contexts, newer font with less institutional adoption

My Recommendation

Nunito Sans for patient-facing areas (forms, appointments, general info)
Source Sans Pro for clinical/administrative areas (charts, data, professional docs)
